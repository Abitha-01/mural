Murals across the world

Ancient Temple Murals

Depict intricate and sacred imagery painted on the walls of temples, often portraying deities, epics, and spiritual themes in vibrant colors and detailed craftsmanship.

Folklore-Themed Murals

Celebrate local legends, traditions, and folk stories, bringing cultural narratives to life through colorful and expressive artwork.

Mythological Depictions

Illustrate epic tales and divine characters from mythology, capturing scenes from Ramayana, Mahabharata, or other spiritual texts with symbolic and elaborate artistry.

True Fresco

A classical painting technique where pigments are applied directly onto freshly laid wet plaster, allowing the colors to fuse into the wall for durability and vibrancy.

Fresco Secco

A technique where pigments mixed with a binding medium are painted on dry plastered walls, offering flexibility but less permanence compared to true fresco.
